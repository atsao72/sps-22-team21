Hello there!

We are a group of software engineering students who are passionate about fitness and keeping track of what activities we do whether it be for fun or for staying healthy and fit. After realizing how sedentary the life of a software engineer can be our group decided there could be an easier way to share and keep progress of our fitness journey. That is why we have created Fitness Gram!

What Does Our App Do?

We are dedicated to allowing our users to share workout routines as well the progress that they have made. Our app makes it possible to connect and share your fitness journey, whether it's for health or mental reasons.
