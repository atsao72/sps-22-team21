What is Bionic Reading?

Bionic Reading is a reading system that supports the readers flow.

The eye is guided through the text by means of typographic highlights.

With the interplay of fixations and the rapid movement between fixation points

The readers visual stimuli can be transferred to the text.

Who is Bionic reading for?

Though Bionic Reading was developed for anyone who has been diagnosed with or identifies with Neurodivergency,

Many who identify with conditions such as ADHD or Dyslexia have found that Bionic Reading improves their reading comprehension.

Though Bionic Reading has helped indivuals with Neurodivergencies, BR is for anyone who finds this method easier to read!
