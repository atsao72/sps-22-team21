Sit Ups

Lie down on your back, with your feet on the floor, knees bent. Place your hands on either side of your head in a comfortable position. Bend your hips and waist to raise your body off the ground. Make sure you keep looking straight ahead, keeping your chin off your chest in a relaxed position. Lower your body back to the ground into the starting position. Repeat

Push ups

Get down on all fours, placing your hands slightly wider than your shoulders. Straighten your arms and legs. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor. Pause, then push yourself back up. Repeat.

guide to running a 5k marathon
